My consumer asks:
I built this list with three lenses in mind: marketing performance, user behavior, and product retention. I added the basics and expanded using our two active pilots (micro-influencer and paid media), our future email needs, and what we'll actually need to make smart decisions as we scale.

That said, you're the expert here and I've no doubt there are things worth adding, reframing, or flagging as more complex than they appear. Items marked with an asterisk are ones I'd especially love your input on. Happy to walk through this together once you're ready--no doubt you'll need clarification on where these will be coming from!

Consumer Marketing Dashboard Requests
Acquisition

New vs. returning user ratio by channel and campaign
Account creation rate: % of poll engagers who convert to registered users
Account creation by source: which channels and campaigns drive the most signups
Channel performance: CPC, CTR, and cost per new user by source/medium/campaign
Cost per account created (CPA) by channel and campaign
Paid vs. organic split across all views
Campaign-level ROI: total spend vs. accounts created and engaged users
Campaign frequency: how many times the same user sees an ad before converting*
Audience overlap between influencer and paid traffic*
Paid Media

Ad platform data integration: Meta, TikTok, and LinkedIn APIs alongside GA4/UTM data*
Creative format performance: which ad formats drive the strongest results by channel*
UTM and vertical filters available across all views from day one
Influencer

Creator-level performance: individual clicks, CTR, and effective CPC with manual story view input
Influencer tier benchmarking: performance grouped by follower tier (10k-25k, 25k-50k, 50k-75k)
Email

Open rate by campaign type and audience segment
Click-through rate: which emails are driving users back into the product
Click-to-conversion rate: % of email clickers who complete a desired action
Unsubscribe rate by campaign type
List growth rate by acquisition source
Engagement & Product Behavior

Poll completion rate with drop-off point tracking by vertical: started vs. finished and which verticals lose users fastest
Poll vote distribution: A vs. B split per poll
Time-to-vote: how quickly users engage once they land
Vertical performance: side-by-side engagement metrics across Sports, Food, and Lifestyle
Content-level performance: which poll topics and hooks drive the highest engagement
Share and virality rate: how often users share polls after voting by vertical
Session depth: how many polls a user engages with in a single session
Retention

User return behavior: Day 1, Day 7, Day 30 retention rates filterable by acquisition channel including email
Repeat engagement: % of users who vote more than once and frequency of return
Churn indicators: users who were active and have gone quiet
Notification engagement rate: % of users returning via push or email notifications
Conversion event tracking: downstream actions after voting (sign up, share, return)*
Feature usage by cohort: which acquisition source or vertical cohorts are most engaged long-term*
User Journey & Behavior

User journey mapping: path from first touch to account creation by channel and content type*
Peak engagement times: most active days and hours by vertical and channel, including best send times for email
Traffic quality: bounce rate and time-on-site by channel and vertical
Funnel drop-off: where users exit before completing a vote, tracked by channel and creative source
Audience

Audience demographics: age, device, OS, and geo breakdown with US vs. non-US segmentation


*May require additional build complexity? Prioritize after core views are stable.
